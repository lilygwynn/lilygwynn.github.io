Honey Dipper Lamp for mawa.

An intuitive, multipurpose table lamp that fits the aesthetic of mawa in Potsdam, Germany. The lamp is inspired by honey dippers. The design explores the use of layered shades affecting the light and shadow. The design is aluminum powder-coated gold except for the battery-powered lighting elements. The lamp was made in Fusion 360 and the table with environment is created in Blender.
